Condor

History of Creation

Inspired by commercial lettering and signage from the late 1920s and 30s. Graphic designer David Jonathan Ross created Condor, the high-contrast, sans-serif type family, in 2010. The art deco look of condor inspires designers today, with the font appeaing in many works trying to call bak to that time. Condors large variety of styles, weights, and widths make it a popular choice for designers trying to show off luxury, energy, and an art deco look in their work.
